How it works

1. Prepare your design

Create or export your design as a compatible file (DWG, DXF, PDF, STL and other supported formats). Make sure your dimensions and layers are clearly defined.

2. Choose material & finish

Select from Mild Steel, Stainless Steel (304, 316, 430, 3CR12) and Aluminium 1050, in a range of surface finishes and thicknesses. Our quote form guides you through the options.

3. Request your quote

Fill in the Request a Quote form with your details, attach your design file, and submit. We’ll review and email you a detailed quotation.

4. We laser cut your parts

Once you accept the quote, we schedule your job, laser cut your parts according to your specification, and prepare them for dispatch.

5. Delivery or collection

Your finished parts are packed and sent to your specified address, or made available for collection, depending on your preference.
