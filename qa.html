Q & A

QuestionAnswer
What are your tolerances? Cutting: within 0.25mm. Bending: +/- 1mm typical (communicate critical bends).
Key Parameters:
  • Min bend radius: soft materials ≈ 1× thickness; harder materials ≈ ≥2× thickness.
  • Min flange length: 4× thickness or 12mm.
  • Feature spacing: ≥4× thickness from bend lines.
  • Bend angle tolerance: ±1° | Bend length: ±0.20mm per bend.
  • ISO 2768 referenced for general tolerances.
Tips for effective bending? Avoid very acute (<130°) or extreme obtuse (>175°) angles. Space successive bends. Consider material hardness and yield strength.
What metals do you offer? Mild Steel, 3CR12, 430, 304, 316 Stainless Steels, Aluminium 1050. Reach out for unlisted materials.
How do I design a part? See How it works. Provide shape, dimensions, or drawing with critical measurements. Learn CAD for iterative control.
How do returns work? Custom parts produced to approved design & tolerance generally not returnable for suitability. If we make an error we replace at no cost.
Surface finishes differences?
  • Hot Rolled: rough scale, cheaper.
  • Cold Rolled: smooth, precise, stronger.
  • Galvanised: zinc-coated corrosion resistance.
  • 2D / No.1 / BA / 2B: various stainless finishes (matte to bright).
  • PVC variants: protective film.
  • No.4 PVC: uniform brushed, repairable.
